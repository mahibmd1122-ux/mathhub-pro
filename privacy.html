Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

At MathHub Pro (accessible from mathhub.pro), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by MathHub Pro and how we use it.

1. Log Files

MathHub Pro follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

2. Google AdSense & The DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to www.mathhub.pro and other sites on the internet.

Visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Third Party Privacy Policies

MathHub Pro's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

4. Consent Management (2026 Update)

In accordance with Google Consent Mode v2, we respect your choices regarding data collection for personalized advertising. Users can manage their preferences at any time through their browser settings or via the "Privacy Settings" link in our footer.

5. Information for Indian Users

As we are based in Mancherial, Telangana, we comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA). We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless you contact us directly via email.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at mathhubpro@gmail.com.
