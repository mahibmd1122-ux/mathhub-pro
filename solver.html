Step 1: Understand the Equation

We are given the equation: 3x + 5 = 20. Our goal is to isolate the variable 'x' on one side of the equation.

Step 2: Subtract Constant

Subtract 5 from both sides of the equation to isolate the term with 'x'.
3x + 5 - 5 = 20 - 5
3x = 15

Step 3: Divide by Coefficient

Divide both sides by 3 to solve for x.
3x / 3 = 15 / 3
x = 5

Final Answer: x = 5
