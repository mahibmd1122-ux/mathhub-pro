Why Use Our Online Unit Converter?

In Class 9 and 10 Physics, many problems require you to convert units before applying a formula. For example, if the radius of a cylinder is in cm but the height is in meters, your volume calculation will be wrong unless you standardize the units first.

Common Conversion Factors

  • 1 Kilometer (km) = 1,000 Meters
  • 1 Meter (m) = 100 Centimeters
  • 1 Centimeter (cm) = 10 Millimeters
